# Privacy Policy — Palm Oracle

**Last updated:** March 25, 2026

Palm Oracle ("we", "our", "the app") is a mobile application that provides palm reading and Vedic Jyotish astrology insights for entertainment and self-reflection purposes. Your privacy is important to us.

## 1. Information We Collect

### Information You Provide
- **Profile data:** Name, date of birth, time of birth, place of birth, gender, and dominant hand. This data is stored locally on your device only.

### Information We Do NOT Collect
- **Palm images:** Your palm photo is processed entirely on-device and is never uploaded, stored, or transmitted to any server. The image is discarded immediately after processing.
- **Reading results:** All palm readings and astrological calculations are generated on-device using on-device AI. No reading data is sent to any external server.

### Automatically Collected Information
- **Advertising data:** Our ad partner, Google AdMob, may collect device identifiers, IP address, and ad interaction data to serve relevant ads. See [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy) for details.
- **Purchase data:** If you make an in-app purchase, Google Play handles all payment processing. We do not collect or store any payment information (credit card numbers, UPI IDs, etc.).
- **Crash and analytics data:** We may collect anonymous crash reports and basic usage analytics to improve the app. This data does not include any personally identifiable information.

## 2. How We Use Information

- **Profile data** is used solely to generate your personalized Jyotish birth chart and palm reading on your device.
- **Advertising data** is used by Google AdMob to display ads within the app.
- We do **not** sell, share, or transfer your personal data to any third party.

## 3. Data Storage

All personal data (name, birth details, reading results) is stored **locally on your device** using SharedPreferences. We do not operate any backend server or cloud database. Uninstalling the app permanently deletes all your data.

## 4. Third-Party Services

The app uses the following third-party services, each with their own privacy policies:

| Service | Purpose | Privacy Policy |

| Google AdMob | Displaying advertisements | [Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy) |
| Google Play Billing | In-app purchases | [Google Play Terms](https://play.google.com/about/play-terms/) |

## 5. Children's Privacy

Palm Oracle is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will delete it.

## 6. Data Retention

Since all data is stored locally on your device:
- Data persists as long as the app is installed.
- Clearing app data or uninstalling the app permanently removes all stored information.
- We retain no data on any server.

## 7. Your Rights

You can at any time:
- **View** your stored profile data within the app.
- **Delete** all your data by clearing the app's data in your device settings or by uninstalling the app.
- **Opt out** of personalized ads via your device's ad settings (Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization).

## 8. Security

All processing (palm reading, astrological calculations, AI inference) happens entirely on your device. No personal data is transmitted over the internet. This on-device architecture is our primary security measure.

## 9. Disclaimer

Palm Oracle is an entertainment and self-reflection application. Readings are generated using palmistry traditions and Vedic Jyotish calculations and should not be considered professional advice of any kind — medical, legal, financial, or otherwise.

## 10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
